This folder contains 6 datasets of minimal pairs:
- 5 datasets with our minimal pairs created by removing negation markers from original XNLI examples in en,de,fr,bg,zh
- 1 dataset of minimal pairs derived from data of [Hossain et al. (20202)] (https://github.com/mosharafhossain/negation-and-nli) based on inserting negation markers into original NLI examples
